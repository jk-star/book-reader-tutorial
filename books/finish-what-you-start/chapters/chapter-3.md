# Utsahit Rehna

Aapko shuru kiye jaane wale kaam ko jaari rakhte hue use poora karne ke liye kaun si cheez utsahit karti hai? Aap kis prakar prerit bane reh sakte hain?

Aaiye Sally naam ki ek mahila ka udaharan lete hain. Sally aadarshvaadi hai. Usne gareebon ki madad karne ke liye ek charity ki shuruaat ki. Use ye ummeed nahi thi ki aisa karne se uske saamne itni saari chunautiyan aayengi. Woh is baat ko nahi samajh paayi ki bina laabh kamaane wala kaam bhi ek business maana jaata hai. Uske kaam mein logon ki madad karne se kahin adhik business shaamil hoga.

Woh apne kaam mein kai chunautiyon ka saamna karti thi. Anya charity sansthaon ke saath donation aur anudaan ke maamle mein uska muqaabla hota tha. Apne uddeshya mein logon ki ruchi banaaye rakhne ke liye use prachaar karna padta tha. Ye sab karte samay woh pareshan ho jaati thi. Woh khud se sawaal karti thi ki logon ko doosron ki parvaah karne ke liye prerit karna itna kathin kyon hai.

Bahut jaldi hi Sally ka apne kaam mein man kam hone laga. Uske kaam mein kai pareshaniyan aur nakaratmak baatein thi. Use madad ke liye logon se paise maangne wale patra likhna aur charity ki yojanaon mein hissa lena achha nahi lagta tha. Jis kaam ko woh dil se karna chahti thi, use chhodne mein use keval kuchh mahine lage.

Doosre log hairaan the ki usne itna mahatvapurn kaam kyon chhod diya. Sally ki asafalta ka ek bada kaaran yeh tha ki woh pehle se aane waali pareshaniyon ko samajh nahi paayi aur unse nipatne ki taiyaari bhi nahi kar saki. Usne logon ki madad karne ke baare mein zyada socha, lekin sanstha chalaane ke liye paise jutaane ke baare mein kam socha.

Woh apne kaam ko poora karne ki prerna ke liye keval apne lakshya par hi nirbhar thi. Isliye usne aisa kuchh nahi kiya jo use kaam ki pareshaniyon aur nakaratmak baaton ka saamna karne mein madad kar sake.

Asal mein Sally ki sabse badi samasya yeh thi ki use sahi prerna nahi mil paayi. Kaam ki pareshaniyan aur nakaratmak baatein uska hausla kam kar deti thi. In mushkilon se baahar nikalne ke liye use kisi sachche protsahan ki zarurat thi. Jab sakaratmak aur nakaratmak baaton ke beech santulan bana rehta hai, tab lakshya tak pahunchne mein aane waali kathinaaiyan aasaan lagne lagti hain.

Sally khud ko yeh yaad dilaakar prerit rakh sakti thi ki usne yeh kaam kyon shuru kiya tha. Woh yeh bhi soch sakti thi ki woh har kaam kis uddeshya se kar rahi hai, yahaan tak ki ve kaam bhi jo use pasand nahi the. Aisa karne se woh apne sapne ko sach kar sakti thi.

Yeh udaharan bataata hai ki keval junoon ke bharose koi kaam poora nahi kiya ja sakta. Kai baar hum apne kaam par sahi dhyaan nahi dete, isliye use poora nahi kar paate. Dheere-dheere ruchi kam hone lagti hai aur utsaah khatam ho jaata hai. Lekin sirf kisi kaam par dhyaan dena hi kaafi nahi hota. Kabhi-kabhi hum un kaamon ko bhi poora nahi kar paate jin par hamaara dhyaan hota hai, kyunki hamaare andar khud ko aage badhaane ki prerna kam ho jaati hai.

Is bhaag mein lekhak samjhaana chahte hain ki jab insaan ke andar prerna kam ho jaati hai, to uska utsaah bhi dheere-dheere khatam hone lagta hai. Iske peeche teen mukhya baatein hoti hain.

**Pehli baat** — Hum apna dhyaan kin cheezon par lagaate hain.

**Doosri baat** — Kisi kaam ko karne se humein kaun-kaun se achhe parinaam milte hain.

**Teesri baat** — Us kaam se judi pareshaniyan ya nakaratmak parinaam, jinse hum bachna chahte hain.

Jab insaan in baaton se juda nahi rehta, to uska man kaam mein nahi lagta aur woh utsaah kho deta hai. Yahi cheezein milkar hamaare andar prerna aur protsahan paida karti hain.

Lekhak aage bataate hain ki protsahan woh cheez hoti hai jo hamaare liye sach mein mahatvapurn hoti hai. Aisi cheezein jo hamaare dil ke kareeb hoti hain, wahi humein apne lakshya ki taraf mehnat karne ke liye prerit karti hain. Yahi baatein humein haar maanane se bhi rokti hain.

Saath hi lekhak yeh bhi kehna chahte hain ki humein apne kaam ki pareshaniyon aur nakaratmak baaton ko kam karne ki koshish karni chahiye aur achhe parinaamon par zyada dhyaan dena chahiye. Isse hamaara utsaah bana rehta hai.

Ant mein lekhak bataate hain ki protsahan do prakaar ka ho sakta hai —

**Ek baahari protsahan** aur **doosra aantarik protsahan**. Yaanee kabhi humein baahar ki cheezein prerit karti hain aur kabhi hamaare andar ki ichchha aur soch humein aage badhaati hai.
